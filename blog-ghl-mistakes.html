5 GoHighLevel Mistakes That Are Costing You Leads Every Day

By Samuel Peter · February 15, 2026 · 6 min read
← Back to Blog

GoHighLevel is one of the most powerful CRM and marketing automation platforms available today. But after working with dozens of businesses across the US, UK, and Canada, I keep seeing the same mistakes over and over — mistakes that silently drain leads and kill revenue every single day.

The frustrating part? Most of these are easy to fix once you know what to look for. Here are the 5 most common GoHighLevel mistakes I see, and exactly how to fix them.

01

No Follow-Up Automation After Lead Opt-In

This is the #1 mistake. A lead fills out your form or books a call — and nothing happens automatically. No confirmation email. No follow-up text. No nurture sequence. They just sit in your CRM going cold.

The Fix: Build a simple 3-step automation triggered immediately when a lead opts in: (1) instant SMS confirmation, (2) email with what to expect, (3) follow-up text 24 hours later if they haven't responded. This alone can increase your show-up rate by 40–60%.

02

Pipeline Stages That Don't Match Your Sales Process

Most people set up GHL with the default pipeline stages and never customize them. The result? Your team doesn't know what action to take at each stage, leads get stuck, and deals fall through the cracks.

The Fix: Map your actual sales process first — every step from first contact to closed deal. Then build your GHL pipeline to match it exactly. Each stage should have a clear definition and a clear next action. Automations should fire when a lead moves from one stage to the next.

03

Emails Landing in Spam Due to Poor Setup

If you haven't configured your domain's DNS records properly — specifically SPF, DKIM, and DMARC — your GoHighLevel emails are likely landing in spam. You could be sending beautiful campaigns that nobody ever reads.

The Fix: Log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) and add the proper DNS records that GHL provides in the settings. Then use a tool like mail-tester.com to check your spam score before sending any campaign. A properly configured domain can take your open rates from 8% to 35%+.

04

Broken Workflows With No Error Handling

GoHighLevel workflows are powerful but fragile if not set up correctly. I regularly audit accounts where workflows are firing at the wrong time, sending duplicate messages, or failing silently because a condition is broken.

The Fix: Audit every active workflow in your account. Check the history/logs to see if they're firing correctly. Add wait steps and conditions to prevent duplicate messages. Set up a simple test lead to run through every workflow before it goes live.

05

Not Using the Conversation Inbox Properly

GHL's unified inbox is one of its best features — it combines SMS, email, Facebook, Instagram, and live chat in one place. But most businesses either ignore it completely or don't have it properly connected to their channels.

The Fix: Connect all your communication channels to the GHL inbox. Set up auto-responses for after-hours messages. Assign conversations to specific team members. Use the AI conversation assistant to qualify leads automatically before a human takes over.

The Bottom Line

GoHighLevel is only as powerful as the person who sets it up. A poorly configured GHL account is worse than no CRM at all — because it gives you a false sense of security while leads silently fall through the cracks.

If you're not sure whether your GoHighLevel account is set up correctly, the first step is an honest audit. Look at your workflows, check your pipeline, test your email deliverability, and follow a lead through every step of your funnel.
